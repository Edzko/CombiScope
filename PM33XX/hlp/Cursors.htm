Usign the Info Windows and the Cursors

The Cursors

The PM33XX Application supports horizontal and/or vertical cursors. The horizontal cursors slide across the vertical axis, and are used to analyze the voltage levels of the signals. The vertical cursors slide across the time axis and are used to analyze timing and frequency properties of the signals.

Select a combination of cursors from the Cursors drop-down button in the main applicaiton ribbon. The colors of the cursors can be adjusted by clicking on the specific cursor slider, and then adjusting th ecolor wiht the color selection button in the main ribbon.

The Info Windows

Additional information about the signals and the cursors is available in the Info windows. The Info windows appear on the right of the graphical signals display, and are color-coded to match the signal the information is referring to.
