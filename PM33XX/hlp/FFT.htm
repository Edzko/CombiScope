Frequency Analysis of active signals

Click on the "Spectrum" button in the application ribbon to switch to the Frequency analysis mode.

Windowing Functions:

A series of windowsing functions are avalable for the discrete Fourier Transform procedure.

  • Rectangular (No function)
  • Gauss
  • Hamming
  • Hann
  • Bartlett
  • Welch
  • Triangle
  • Bartlett-Hann
  • Blackman
  • Kaiser-Bessel
  • Nutall
  • Blackman-Harris
  • Blackman-Nutall
  • Flat top 
  • Sine

More can be found in M. Bosi & R.E. Goldberg, "Introduction to Digital Audio Coding and Standards", Springer, 2003, ISBN: 978-1-4020-7357-1.
