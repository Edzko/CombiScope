Capturing signals and Hardcopy from the CombiScope

Captuing Signals

First, select the Channels and Memory banks to capture in the "Traces and Channels" ribbon. Then click on "Get Traces". Based on the baudrate, the transfer of the data from the CombiScope to PM33XX may take some time. Monitor progress in the Status bar of the interface window.

Capturing a Hardcopy

You can capture a snapshot of the current screen of the CombiScope as a direct image using the HardCopy function. When the screen is configured how you like, click on the "HardCopy" button.

Note: the progress indicator in the status bar is not very accurate. The progress can also be monitored on the screen of the CombiScope.
